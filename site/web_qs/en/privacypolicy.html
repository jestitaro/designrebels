Introduction

Welcome to our Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy will help you make better decisions about your relationship with us, so we ask you to read it carefully.

Before going into details, we highlight a few key principles this Policy is based on. These principles are important to us because we know they are important to you.

Goals of this Policy

  • Explain clearly how we use the information you share with us to build a great product that provides an excellent experience for you and your company.
  • Ensure you understand what information we collect with your permission and what we do, and do not do, with it.
  • Hold ourselves accountable for protecting your rights and privacy under this Policy.

All the information we collect is related to providing the QuartzSales service and its features as contracted. Each QuartzSales instance corresponds to the services requested and agreed with our Clients.

Information categories

In general terms, there are two categories of information we collect: (1) information we must have so that you and your Company can use QuartzSales and (2) information we may use to provide additional features and better experiences.

Information needed to use QuartzSales

  • Registration and user information.
  • Data you receive and enter into the application, such as text, images, audio, videos, etc.
  • Precise location, which we obtain from the device’s GPS, Wi-Fi networks and mobile networks.
  • Technical and sensor information needed to operate QuartzSales (browser and device type, touchscreen data, accelerometer and gyroscope) for functions such as screen rotation, among others.

Information to provide additional features

We will collect it only with your explicit permission.

  • Device microphone. We will never access or use it unless you grant explicit permission. This could allow you to control QuartzSales by voice. You can disable access at any time.

The distinction between these two categories is important. The information in the first category is necessary to use QuartzSales. By accepting this Policy, you grant us the right to collect and use it for the purposes described.

The information in the second category will only be collected if you grant explicit permission. Before the first access, we will request permission, describe the intended use, limit its use to that purpose, and you may revoke permission whenever you wish.

Accepting this Policy does not mean you have granted us access to information in the second category; we only explain that we may request it in the future.

Our commitment

We hope this helps you understand the privacy commitments we have with you. This Introduction is part of the Privacy Policy itself and is our promise to you.
